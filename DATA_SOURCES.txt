DATA SOURCES
============
Public databases used in this project. NOT included in the repository due to size;
download from the sources below to reproduce the analysis.

1. Pig Virome Database (PVD) - primary viral input
   Reference: Mi et al. (2024), npj Biofilms and Microbiomes 10:76
   Content: 5,566,804 viral contigs / 48,299 vOTUs from 4,650 pig gut metagenomes
   Use: source of viral contigs; filtered here with CheckV (completeness > 90%)

2. NCBI nr (non-redundant protein database)
   Use: DIAMOND blastp reference
   Access: https://ftp.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/blast/db/

3. CheckV database (v1.5)
   Reference: Nayfach et al. (2021), Nat Biotechnol
   Access: https://portal.nersc.gov/CheckV/

4. CheckAMG database (v1_20260128)
   Use: AVG identification + functional annotation
   Access: AnantharamanLab / CheckAMG (GitHub)

5. iPHoP database (v1.4)
   Reference: Roux et al. (2023), PLoS Biol 21:e3002083
   Access: https://bitbucket.org/srouxjgi/iphop/

6. geNomad database
   Reference: Camargo et al. (2024), Nat Biotechnol
   Access: https://portal.nersc.gov/genomad/

7. PhaGCN3
   Use: viral taxonomic classification (high-confidence predictions)
   Access: PhaGCN / PhaBOX (GitHub)

8. Bacterial reference genomes (self-assembled set)
   Content: 179 complete RefSeq genomes, 20 pig-gut genera across 5 phyla
   Download: via NCBI 'datasets' tool (see download_sampled.sh in code/)
   Note: after geNomad prophage removal -> 511,333 clean bacterial proteins
